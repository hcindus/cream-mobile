## 📞 Sales Pitch Scripts

### 30-Second Elevator Pitch

> "Hi [Name], I'm [Your Name] with CREAM. I know you're probably using 4-5 different tools to manage your real estate business - CRM, marketing, financial tracking... CREAM replaces all of that with one simple platform for $699 one-time. No monthly fees. Want to see how much you could save?"

### 2-Minute Discovery Call

**Opening:**
> "Thanks for taking my call. I'm reaching out because we built CREAM specifically for real estate agents who are tired of paying multiple monthly subscriptions. Can I ask - how many different tools are you currently paying for to run your business?"

**Discovery Questions:**
1. "What CRM are you currently using? How much does it cost per month?"
2. "Are you doing any automated marketing or email campaigns? What platform?"
3. "How do you track your commissions and expenses right now?"
4. "Do you do any community farming or neighborhood marketing?"
5. "How do you schedule appointments and track follow-ups?"

**Value Calculation:**
> "So that's [CRM] at $49, [marketing] at $79, [financial] at $29... That's already $157 per month or $1,884 per year. With CREAM, you'd pay $699 once and $99 per year - that's a **savings of over $2,000 in the first year alone** and over $1,800 every year after."

**Close:**
> "When would be a good time for me to show you the platform? It takes about 15 minutes, and I can answer any questions you have."

### Objection Handling

**"I already have a CRM"**
> "I totally understand - and if it's working perfectly for you, keep it. But most agents I talk to find they're paying for features they don't use, or they need to export data to other tools. CREAM combines CRM with marketing, financial tracking, and appointment management - so you get more value for less money. Can I show you the difference?"

**"$699 is a lot upfront"**
> "I hear that. Let me ask - what are you paying monthly right now? [Let them answer] So at $X/month, CREAM pays for itself in just [calculate months]. After that, you're saving $X every single month. Plus, you own it - there's no contract, no recurring fees to worry about."

**"I need to think about it"**
> "Of course. When you think about it, what's the main thing you're weighing? Is it the cost, or are you wondering if CREAM has all the features you need?"

**"I'm too busy right now"**
> "I get it - you're probably slammed with clients. That's exactly why CREAM was built - to save agents time by having everything in one place. What if I send you a quick video demo? It takes 5 minutes to watch, and you can decide if it's worth a longer conversation."
