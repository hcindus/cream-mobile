## RED FLAGS (When to Walk Away)

**Don't pursue if:**
- ❌ Prospect wants free forever (no budget)
- ❌ Prospect needs enterprise features (out of scope)
- ❌ Prospect is switching from CREAM (likely unhappy)
- ❌ Prospect has 12+ months left on competitor contract (check back in 6 months)
- ❌ Prospect admits they'll never actually use it (be honest about fit)

**Do pursue if:**
- ✅ Prospect pays for multiple tools currently
- ✅ Prospect complains about current setup
- ✅ Prospect values time over money
- ✅ Prospect wants simplicity
- ✅ Prospect is growing (team or volume)
