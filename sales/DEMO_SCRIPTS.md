## DEMO 1: STARTER TIER (15 Minutes)

### Target Audience
- New agents (0-2 years)
- Part-time agents
- Agents with ≤5 active clients
- Budget-conscious

### Goals
- Show core CRM functionality
- Demonstrate ease of use
- Emphasize cost savings

---

### SCRIPT

**0:00-0:30 - Opening Hook**
> "Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time. I'm going to show you how CREAM can replace the 4-6 tools you're probably using right now—and save you over $2,000 this year. Sound good?"

**0:30-3:00 - Discovery Questions**

Ask these questions to personalize the demo:

1. **"What tools are you using right now to manage your business?"**  
   (Listen for: CRM, spreadsheets, email, etc.)

2. **"How many hours a week do you spend on data entry between systems?"**  
   (Listen for: pain point - usually 5-10 hours)

3. **"What's your biggest frustration with your current setup?"**  
   (Listen for: disorganization, cost, complexity)

**3:00-6:00 - Product Walkthrough**

**Screen Share: Dashboard**

> "This is your CREAM dashboard. Everything in one place."

1. **Contacts (1 minute)**
   - Click "Contacts"
   - Show import feature: "Import from your phone, LinkedIn, or export from anywhere"
   - Demonstrate search: "Find anyone in 2 seconds"
   - Show contact history: "Every touchpoint tracked automatically"

2. **Letter Generator (2 minutes)**
   - Click "Letters"
   - Select "Just Listed" template
   - Show mail merge: "Personalized in seconds"
   - Preview print-ready output
   - Emphasize: "50 templates included"

3. **Financial Tracking (2 minutes)**
   - Click "Finances"
   - Show commission calculator
   - Show expense tracker: "Auto-categorizes receipts"
   - Show P&L: "Ready for your accountant"

**6:00-9:00 - Use Case Scenario**

> "Let me show you a typical day with CREAM..."

Walk through scenario:
1. Morning: Check dashboard for follow-ups
2. Midday: Generate 20 farming letters
3. Afternoon: Log new showing, schedule follow-up
4. Evening: Check P&L, update goals

**9:00-11:00 - ROI Discussion**

> "Let's talk numbers. What are you paying for tools right now?"

Calculate savings:
- Current spend: $[their number]
- CREAM cost: $798 Year 1, $99/year after
- **Savings: $[difference]**

> "So CREAM pays for itself in [X] months, and after that you're saving $[monthly] every month."

**11:00-13:00 - Objection Handling**

**If they say: "I need to think about it"**
> "Totally understand. What's the main thing you're weighing? Is it the cost, or are you wondering if it has all the features you need?"

**If they say: "I'm happy with my current tools"**
> "I hear that. Can I ask—how many hours last week did you spend entering the same data into different systems?"

**If they say: "$699 is a lot upfront"**
003e "It is an investment. Let me ask—if you could get back 5 hours a week, what would that be worth to you?"

**13:00-14:00 - Next Steps**

> "Here's what I recommend..."

Options:
1. **Try it:** "Start your 30-day trial today. Full refund if it's not right."
2. **Think about it:** "I can send you a one-page summary and ROI calculator."
3. **More questions:** "Let's schedule a 30-minute session with your questions ready."

**14:00-15:00 - Close**

> "Thanks for your time, [Name]. I'll send you that [summary/demo link/next steps] within the hour. When's a good time to follow up this week?"
