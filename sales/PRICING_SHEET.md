## FREQUENTLY ASKED PRICING QUESTIONS

**Q: Why is there an annual fee?**  
A: The $99/year covers ongoing updates, security patches, and support. It's optional—you can keep using your current version without updates.

**Q: Is this a subscription?**  
A: No. You own the software forever. The annual fee is for updates only.

**Q: Can I skip the annual fee?**  
A: Yes. You can use your purchased version indefinitely without updates.

**Q: Will the price go up?**  
A: Current customers are price-locked for 3 years. New customers may see price increases.

**Q: Are there hidden fees?**  
A: No. No setup fees, no cancellation fees, no data export fees, no per-contact fees.

**Q: What's not included?**  
A: Domain registration, custom development, printed marketing materials (available as add-ons).
